Blog Post #3 - Scavenger Quest

Posted on: February 24, 2023

At Meeple 4 People, not all fun and games are just on a board. Last weekend our Scavenger Quest event was a huge success! Scavenger Shuffle is an event where customers can participate in a scavenger hunt held in store.

When customers walk into out café and wish to join the hunt, they receive a miniature “scroll.” they must find M4P’s logo in 10 different areas with 2 bonus logos. Once the scroll is received, participants have 1 hour to find all the hidden logos within the store!

Bonus 2 Logos Riddle:

1. I can be full or empty, come in all shapes and sizes, and always have a handle, what am I?

2.I'm often stacked high with food, yet I'm not a shelf, what am I?

All those who have successfully completed the Scavenger Quest win a free 1-hour admission for our café that can be used within the next month. For those who found all the logos including the 2 bonuses, will have a chance to win more prizes such as a M4P gift card, a drink & food combo coupon, or even one of our featured scavenger board games!

Top 10 Scavenger Hunt Theme Board Games

1. Clue

Clue, also known as Cluedo, is a classic murder mystery game that requires players to solve a crime by collecting clues and eliminating suspects. The game board represents a mansion, and players move their pieces through various rooms, searching for clues and interviewing other players to narrow down the suspects.

2. Escape the Room

Escape the Room is a cooperative game where players work together to solve puzzles and unlock hidden objects in order to escape a locked room. The game includes a timer, adding an element of urgency and excitement to the gameplay.

3. The Amazing Labyrinth

In The Amazing Labyrinth, players must navigate a constantly shifting maze in order to collect treasure and reach their goal. The maze is made up of movable tiles, so the game board changes with each turn, making it a unique and challenging game every time

4. Treasure Hunt

Treasure Hunt is a game where players must collect items from various locations around the board in order to win. The game includes challenges and obstacles that players must overcome in order to find the treasure.

5. Sherlock Holmes Consulting

Sherlock Holmes Consulting Detective is a game where players take on the role of detectives and work together to solve a crime. The game includes a book of clues and a map of London, and players must use their deductive reasoning and problem-solving skills to solve the mystery.

6. Spy Alley

In Spy Alley, players take on the role of spies trying to reach their home country without being caught by other players. The game includes bluffing and deception, making it a fun and challenging game for players of all ages.

7. Betrayal at Baldur's Gate

Betrayal at Baldur's Gate is a game where players explore a haunted city and work together to defeat monsters and complete quests. However, halfway through the game, one player becomes the traitor and turns against the others, creating a thrilling and unpredictable gameplay experience.

8. Zombies!!!

Zombies!!! is a game where players must survive a zombie apocalypse and make their way to the helicopter that will take them to safety. The game includes zombie cards, which can be used to attack other players and add an element of danger to the game.

9. The Magic Labyrinth

The Magic Labyrinth, players must navigate a maze without touching the walls or getting lost. The game includes hidden magnets that cause the walls to shift, adding an element of surprise and challenge to the gameplay.

10. Forbidden Island

Forbidden Island is a cooperative game where players work together to collect treasures and escape from a sinking island. The game includes various hazards and obstacles that players must overcome in order to succeed


These are just a few examples of the many scavenger hunt theme board games available. They offer a fun and exciting way to build teamwork, problem-solving skills, and creativity, making them a great choice for game night or family gatherings.
