Game of the month

Feature of the Month: Stardew Valley The Board Game

Dream of living during the simpler days of farming, raising cattle, and building a small community? Introducing the popular RPG game, now available to play as a board game, Stardew Valley: The Board Game! Just like the online game, players will work together to restore and cultivate their grandfather’s old farm. The game can be played with 1-4 players, each one taking up a role (farmer, gatherer, miner, craftsman, and fisherman). The objective is to fulfill all the goals of your late grandfather. Take on the cooperative gameplay and the nostalgic design of the video game!
