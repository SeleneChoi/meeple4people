Blog #6: Monthly Game Recommendations

Posted on: March 17, 2023

Getting tired of playing the same games? At Meeple 4 People, we love sharing our current favorites every month to help you find your next board game! Our staff are excited to share their favourite games as well and we believe that you will find a new favourite too! This month, we will be exploring the top five board games voted by our staff!

1. Terraforming Mars

Terraforming Mars is a popular board game that has been around for a while, but we still think it’s one of the best games to play this month. In this board game, players compete to terraform the planet Mars by raising the temperature, oxygen, and ocean levels. Players must use cards to build infrastructure, plant forests, and create oceans to make the planet habitable. The game is played over several rounds, and the player with the most victory points at the end of the game wins. The game is perfect for players who enjoy strategy games with a science-fiction theme. There are six types of resources available for players to track: MegaCredits, Steel, Titanium, Plants, Energy, and Heat. Players need to also keep track of global parameters such as temperature, oxygen, and ocean. In the end, you will count your Terraform Rating and victory points to see who won!

2. Ticket to Ride Japan

Ticket to Ride is a classic board game that has been around for over a decade, and it's still a game that's worth playing. In this version of the game, players travel through Japan building train routes between cities. The game is played over several rounds, and players must collect train cards and use them to claim routes on the board. The player with the most victory points at the end of the game wins. Ticket to Ride is a game that is perfect for players who enjoy train-themed strategy games.

3. Oath

Oath is a new board game that's perfect for players who enjoy games with a fantasy theme. In this game, players compete to become the ruler of a kingdom, with each player taking on a different role. Players must use cards and tokens to build influence, claim territory, and gain supporters. However, the game is not just about accumulating power; players must also make alliances, deal with changing political landscapes, and make promises that they may not be able to keep. The game is played over several rounds, and the player with the most points at the end of the game wins.

4. Tiny Towns

Tiny Towns is a simple but addictive board game that's perfect for players who enjoy city-building games. In this game, players take turns placing building blocks on a 4x4 grid. Each block represents a different building, such as a farm, tavern, or bakery. Players must strategically place the blocks to create patterns that will earn them victory points. However, players must also be careful not to overbuild, as they may run out of space. The game is played over several rounds, and the player with the most points at the end of the game wins.

5. Bunny Kingdom

Bunny Kingdom is a whimsical board game that's perfect for players who enjoy games with a cute theme. In this game, players take on the role of rabbit lords, building their kingdoms in a world of magic and adventure. Players must collect resources, build cities, and recruit armies of bunny warriors to defend their territories. The game is played over several rounds, and the player with the most points at the end of the game wins. The game is perfect for players who enjoy light strategy games with a fantasy theme.

In conclusion, these are the top five board games of the month. Each of these games offers something unique and exciting for players whether they enjoy strategy games, city-building games, or games with a fantasy or sci-fi theme. So, gather your friends and family, choose your favorite game, and have fun!

Come on out to Meeple 4 People Cafe to play these awesome games! They are sure to be a crowd pleaser, but don’t take our word for it. Try them out for yourselves and let us know what you think!
