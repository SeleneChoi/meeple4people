"Growing healthy communities by giving low-income families access to locally grown food."


- B.C. Vancouver Farmer's Market Association

Giving even $40 can help a whole family with fresh farm food


With the rise of food costs and declining economy, families are struggling to meet their basic needs. Many have no choice but to compromise their health and well-being. With your help, your donation can empower a family make simple decisions to healthier food. Help families gain access to locally grown produce, meat, eggs, and dairy.
