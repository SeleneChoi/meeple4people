How do Coupons work?

Our coupons allow almost 400 families to shop stress-free at our farmers market. Recipients can use the coupons to save up to half the cost of their groceries!

Coupon Benefits

  • Support 82 farmers in B.C. and improve agriculture.
  • Encourages positive environmental impact,
  • reducing ecological footprint.
  • Connect communities and neighbouring farmers.
