About Us


Fresh to Families is a community initiative lead by the BCAFM (British Columbia Association of Farmers Market). Today, not everyone has the financial ability to access these foods. Many families including lowincome households, new refugees, and single parents or expectant parents face trouble in maintaining healthy meals on their tables.


In our program, we provide food coupons for these families, giving stress-free experience to food shopping. Along with the coupon program, news, events, and educational programs are made to foster our nutritional campaign. We believe good, healthy food comes right here, from the best of our Vancouver farmers. Joining hands with our local farmers and supportive members, we strive to nurture our neighbouring families.
