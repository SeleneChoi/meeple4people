Our Story

Welcome to our Meeple 4 People Cafe!

Our story began with four friends who shared a love for board games. Chrisitne, Nicola, Selene, and Julie have been friends since college, and they have always dreamed of starting their own business together. They were passionate about board games and loved the idea of creating a space where people could gather and enjoy playing games with friends and family.

Over the span of four years of development in research, planning, and designing the perfect space for their vision, the cafe opened its doors in February of 2023. Meeple 4 People is created to create a cozy and comfortable atmosphere where people could relax, play games, and enjoy delicious snacks and drinks. For board game selection, a wide range can be found from classics, competitive, strategic, modern and rare finds, to cater to all types of gamers.

Located in Downtown Vancouver, our cafe is the destination for game lovers. A new community filled with fun and joy is welcomed for all guests . Meeple 4 People also organizes game nights, tournaments, and other events to bring more people together and make the cafe a hub for gaming culture.

The board game cafe is not just a business, but a passion born from the wonderful memories of Christine, Nicola, Selene, and Julie. Meeple 4 People will continue to work hard to maintain the highest standards of quality and service and provide the best possible experience for their customers. Thank you for visiting our board game cafe, and we hope you enjoy your time here!
