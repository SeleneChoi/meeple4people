Blog #5 - Workshops

Posted on: March 10, 2023

When it comes to board games, there are various components that make gameplay an amazing experience. These components include the initial game concept, design, and mechanics. When passion and detail is given to these three components , some of the most immersive games are born.

At M4P, we don’t just love playing board games. We love sharing the art of board games by exploring all aspects of this fantastic world! We’re excited to announce that we are offering monthly workshops where board-game enthusiasts can learn to build their very own board-game world!

Workshop Level 1: Design Your Dice

Have you played many dice-base games but are tired of using the same old boring dice? Join us for our Level 1 workshop, “Design Your Dice!” an exciting event where you can learn how to make your own custom board game dice!

Our event will be led by Julie Kim, a Vancouver-based mixed media artist that specializes in wood, resin, and synthetic materials, and a board game enthusiast herself! Julie will guide you through the process of designing and creating your own set of unique dice using a variety of materials. You'll learn how to craft dice of different shapes and sizes, and even add your own personalized designs and markings.

Event Highlights:

  • Learn how to make custom board game dice from scratch
  • Use a variety of materials to create your own unique design
  • Add personalized markings and symbols to your dice
  • Meet other board game enthusiasts and share your creations
  • Enjoy snacks and refreshments while you craft your dice

Whether you're a seasoned board game enthusiast or just looking for a fun and creative activity, this is the perfect event for you! Join us and unleash your imagination to create the perfect set of dice for your favorite board games.

Workshop Level 2: “PawnCraft: Create your Miniature”

Calling all Dungeon and Dragon players! Join us for DnD PawnCraft, a fun and interactive event where you can learn how to make your own custom pawns!

Featuring Christine and Nicola Lee, two board game specialists who love bringing life to their DnD characters. They will guide you through the process of designing and creating your own set of unique pawns by teaching you how to assemble and paint your miniatures to perfection!

In this workshop we will be using model pieces and paint sets by The Army Painter.

Event Highlights:

  • Learn how to sketch your custom Dungeons and Dragons pawns
  • Use miniature pieces to assemble your own unique designs
  • Add personalized details and features to your pawns
  • Meet other DnD players and share your creations
  • Enjoy snacks and refreshments while you craft your pawns

Whether you're a seasoned DnD player or just starting out, bring your character to life at our workshop! Join us and unleash your creativity to create the perfect pawns for all your epic adventures!

Workshop Level 3: “Build Your Board Game”

Join us for our "Build Your Own Board Game Workshop'' where you can create your very own board game from scratch. Let loose your creativity and design a game that is uniquely yours.

Board game engineer Selene Choi will guide you through the basics of storytelling and mechanics. You will receive guidance on how to bring your game to life using a range of design tools and materials. Participants will have the opportunity to brainstorm and pitch your game idea, design game mechanics and rules, and finally build a game using a variety of materials.

At the end of the workshop, you will have created a custom board game that you can take home and play with friends and family. If you have always wondered how a board game is made, this is an exciting opportunity to explore your creative side and learn more about the world of board game design.

Event Highlights:

  • Brainstorming session to pitch game ideas and receive feedback
  • Game design phase with access to design tools and expert guidance
  • Construction phase with access to materials to create a board game
  • Test your game and provide feedback on other games
  • Networking with other board game enthusiasts and designers
  • Bonus prizes for the most creative and well-designed games.

How can I join a Workshop?

Depending on the date of our workshops, times and signup costs will vary. Keep checking in on our website and subscribe to our newsletter! Our workshops will be offered on alternating months throughout the year so you don’t have to worry about missing out. Stay tuned for more, and we hope to see you there!
